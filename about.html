About Morgenrot Studio

Hi, I’m Vanessa, the designer behind Morgenrot Studio. This space represents my creative work in graphic design, focusing on branding, typography, and visual storytelling.

Morgenrot Studio was founded with a simple goal: to create original visual work that communicates ideas clearly and thoughtfully. My process often begins with research and sketching, followed by experimentation with composition, type, and color until a concept feels complete.

The name “Morgenrot,” meaning “dawn” or “morning red,” reflects the idea of new beginnings and creative growth. Each project represents a step forward in my development as a designer.
